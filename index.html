About Me

As software engineers we have a wealth of technology at our fingertips, putting us in the unique position of having nearly limitless possibilities to what we apply that technology to. Social impact engineering is one such way we can assume the responsibility of creating products that not only benefit the more privileged users of technology, but also address the fundamental needs of those less fortunate. Through my involvement in organizations like Cal Blueprint and The Berkeley Group, I've had firsthand experience working with non-profits and gained a deeper understanding of the necessity of social impact engineering, and I hope to continue working on meaningful projects post-graduation at a mission-focused company.
