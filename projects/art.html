Portfolio

I took art lessons from kindergarten to my senior year of high school, during which I developed a solid foundation in graphite, watercolor, pastel, and oil. In high school I was awarded a full scholarship to attend Ryman Arts, a program created by Herbert D. Ryman, one of the original Disney Imagineers, where I was taught new mediums like acrylic and conte crayon by professional artists. Art has played a significant role in my formative years, below is a portfolio of some of my favorite pieces.
