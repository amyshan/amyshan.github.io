Graphic Design

Portfolio

In college I didn't have the time or materials to continue fine arts, and I transitioned to graphic design instead. I learned Illustrator and Photoshop my first semester and worked on small projects like creating two Snapchat Geofilters that are currently live in Redondo Beach and Berkeley. I was a part of a campus club called Innovative Design, where I was commissioned by other campus organizations like the Transfer Student Center or the Cal Solar Car team to design logos for them.
